DeckChat is a secure, hands-free voice interface to AI crew members.

Voice is captured on Android via Bluetooth headset and converted to text entirely on-device using Sherpa-ONNX (offline, Apache 2.0). No audio ever leaves your phone.

Messages are sent via E2EE Matrix to your self-hosted homeserver and answered by AI crew members in their own voices, also synthesised on-device using Piper TTS.

Features:
- Offline speech-to-text (Whisper via Sherpa-ONNX — no cloud AI)
- On-device text-to-speech with crew voice profiles (Piper ONNX)
- End-to-end encrypted messaging via Matrix
- Headset button support (push-to-talk)
- Bluetooth audio routing
- No Google Play Services required
- Self-hosted — you control the server

Privacy: voice processing is entirely on-device. No audio, transcripts, or conversation data leave the phone.
